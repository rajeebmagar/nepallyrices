Proudly supporting and promoting Nepali Music!

Nepalyrics (Nepali + Lyrics) is an open social platform driven by community and for community of Nepali music enthusiasts. A platform where user can contribute their favourite Nepali songs/lyrics/artists/photographs. Share it among friends, follow friends and artists.

Being an open platform users' can contribute the songs audio, lyrics and artists as well as photographs of songs and artists. It provides an easy way to synchronize an audio with lyrics, just by listening an audio and tapping at times. That's it.
